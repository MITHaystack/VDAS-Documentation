Mark6 VLBI Data Acquisition System¶

Welcome to the Mark6 website! This is where you can find answers to all things related to the Mark6 VLBI Data Acquisition System. If you have any questions, comments or suggestions, we would love to hear from you! Please email us: del@haystack.mit.edu.

The Mark6 VLBI Data Acquisition System is the next generation, high speed VLBI Data Acquisition System (DAS) jointly developed by MIT Haystack Observatory and NASA Goddard Space Flight Center High End Computer Network Team.
