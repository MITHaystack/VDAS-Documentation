Demonstrations¶

On Monday, October 24th, 2011 a two antenna, a successful 16 Gbps experiment was run between Westford Radio Telescope Westford

in Westford, MA, and the

Goddard Geophysical and Astronomical Observatory (GGAO) Radio Telescope in Greenbelt, MD.

The successful demonstration was the result of a lot of hard work from folks at MIT Haystack Observatory and NASA Goddard Space Flight Center High End Computer Network Team.

Results¶

Figures 1 through 3 show the results of the 16 Gbps demonstration.
