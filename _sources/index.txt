Mark6 VLBI Data Acquisition System
==================================

Welcome to the Mark6 wiki! This is where you can find answers to all things
related to the Mark6 VLBI Data Acquisition System.
If you have any questions or comments, we would love to hear from you.
Please email us: del@haystack.mit.edu.

The Mark6 VLBI Data Acquisition System is the next generation, high speed
VLBI Data Acquisition System (DAS) jointly developed by MIT Haystack
Observatory and NASA Goddard Space Flight Center High End Computer Network
Team.
